About

My name is Hanna Johansson and on this page I've presented some information about me.

Soon graduating..

I am currently studying my 5th year of my M.Sc. in Media Technology and Engineering at Linköping University, campus Norrköping.

My master thesis is however done at the Scientific Computing and Imaging (SCI) Institute at the University of Utah as a part of the open-source software project OpenSpace.

It is a project between the collaborators Linköping University (LiU), NASA Goddard’s Community Coordinated Modeling Center (CCMC), American Museum of Natural History (AMNH), New York University’s (NYU) Polytechnic School of Engineering and University of Utah’s SCI Institute.

Background

During my studies at LiU I have been engaged in several associations and events.

Among other things I have been on the Board of the Media Technology program, part of the Welcomming Committee for LinTek (the Student Union for the Institute of Technology at LiU) and a student ambassador for the competition C Awards.

As part of my interests of trying new things, meeting new people and travelling I decided to spend my 4th year of university studies as an exchange student abroad. My exchange year was spent on the French Riviera at Eurecom, Sophia-Antipolis, taking courses in computer science, management and leadership among other subjects.

Work

As part of several courses during my studies I have been involved in working in projects, most of which have been developed through GitHub.

Some of the projects that I have enjoyed the most are developing an Android mobile application in the course "Communication and User Interfaces (TNM040)", developing a web application visualizing articles on Wikipedia in the Bachelor Project course TNM094 and developing a user experience in a museum environment in the course "Advanced Interaction Design (TDDD53)".

Web development, visualization, mobile applications, user experience and interaction design are interests in general.
